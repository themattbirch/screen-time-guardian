Screen Time Guardian Launch App

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Data Collection and Usage

Screen Time Guardian collects and stores only the following data locally on your device:

  • Timer preferences and settings
  • Achievement progress
  • Theme preferences
  • Sound settings

No personal information is collected, transmitted, or shared with third parties.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally using your device's storage capabilities and remains on your device. We use standard web storage APIs and do not use external servers or databases.

3. App Permissions

Screen Time Guardian requires the following permissions:

  • Storage access: To save your settings and preferences locally
  • Notification permissions: To send timer completion alerts
  • Wake lock: To prevent the device from sleeping during active sessions (optional)

4. Offline Usage

As a Progressive Web App, Screen Time Guardian works offline and stores necessary assets locally. This includes app resources, icons, and quotes database. No data is transmitted during offline usage.

5. Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Users will be notified of any significant changes through the app or this website.
