Get help with Mindful Browsing Timer

Getting Started

  1. Click the extension icon to open the timer
  2. Select your preferred timer mode:
    • Focus (25 minutes)
    • Short Break (5 minutes)
    • Long Break (15 minutes)
    • Custom duration
  3. Click "Start" to begin your session

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I pause the timer?

A: Click anywhere on the window when the timer is running to pause it. Click the resume button to continue.

Q: The sound isn't working. What should I do?

A: Check the following:

  • Verify that sounds are enabled in the extension settings
  • Check your system volume
  • Ensure Chrome has permission to play sounds
Q: How do I change the theme?

A: Click the sun/moon icon in the top-left corner to switch between light and dark themes.
